Martingales
-----------
Definition

.. math::
   E(|X_{n}|) < \infty

.. math::
   E(X_{n+1} \mid X_{1, \ldots, X_{n}}) = X_{n}

----

Submartingale

.. math::
   E(X_{n+1} \mid X_{1, \ldots, X_{n}}) \geq X_{n}

----

Supermartingale

.. math::
   E(X_{n+1} \mid X_{1, \ldots, X_{n}}) \leq X_{n}

----

Optimal Stopping Theorem
